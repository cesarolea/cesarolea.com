Rockofonia

Rockofonia was a podcast centered around Hard Rock and Heavy Metal. It spanned two seasons with more than 200 episodes, and for a little while it even was syndicated in the BC México area. Episodes either feature Tony GM or myself as DJ.

Now in Spanish
